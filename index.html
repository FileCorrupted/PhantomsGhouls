Welcome to Phantoms and Ghouls

Phantoms and Ghouls is a project heavily inspired by Ghosts and Goblins. The game will seek to emulate the fun Ghosts and Goblins provided players. Features and mechanics are planned for the future that were not in Ghosts and Goblins. Currently the game is being made by one guy (I keep him locked up in my basement)
